# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful learning environment for people of every
language background, experience level, identity, and ability.

Expected behavior includes:

- giving specific, evidence-based feedback;
- correcting language without humiliating the learner;
- challenging ideas rather than attacking people;
- acknowledging uncertainty and mistakes;
- respecting copyright, attribution, and privacy.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks,
deliberate misinformation, plagiarism, and publication of private information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation
when behavior violates these standards. Report conduct concerns privately to
the repository owner through their GitHub profile.

Maintainers should handle reports consistently, protect reporter privacy where
possible, and document moderation decisions.
